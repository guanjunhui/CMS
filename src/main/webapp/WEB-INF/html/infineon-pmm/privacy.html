4.Cookies

4.1Cookies

所有英飞凌网站都使用 cookie。Cookie 是存储在您的计算机上的一个小文本文件。Cookie 可以让您的浏览器识别您和您网站上的最新设置（例如语言设置）。您的设置将立即可用，不必再次进行设置。同时，我们也可以为您提供量身定制的服务。

4.2网站分析服务

我们可能使用第三方或者自身研发的网站分析服务，例如百度统计。这些服务供应商使用Cookie、网络服务器记录以及其他科技帮助我们分析访问者如何使用本站。通过这些方式(包括IP地址)收集的信息会披露给这些服务提供者，他们使用这些信息来评估网站的使用。

4.3您不想在您的计算机上保存cookie?

有些用户不希望网站在他们的计算机上存储数据 - 特别是如果这些数据是在他们不知情的情况下被第三方收集和使用 - 即使这通常没有任何进一步的后果。针对这种情况，您可以选择阻止单个或所有 cookie，或者删除已存储的 cookie。但是，我们想指出，这可能会影响您在我们网站的上网体验。

4.3.1阻止第三方 Cookie

来自第三方的 cookie 通常不需要使用您要求的互联网服务。如果您想在互联网上留下更少的痕迹，您通常应该拒绝这些 cookie。

4.3.2限制 Adobe Flash 中的跟踪

建议您使用在线设置管理器重新定义访问保护设置。但是，如果您完全拒绝访问，Flash 将再也无法正常工作。

4.Cookies

4.1Cookies

All Infineon websites use cookies. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your computer. A cookie enables your browser to recognize you and your most recent settings (e. g. language settings) on the website. Your settings will be made available immediately and don't have to be done again. At the same time, we can also offer you tailor-made offers

4.2Website Analytics

We may use the third party or independent website analytics service, like Baidu Analytics. These service providers will help to analyze how the visitors use website through Cookies, web-service log and other technologies. The collected information (include IP address) will be forwarded to service provider, they will use these information to evaluate the usage of Website.

4.3You do not want cookies on your computer?

Some users do not want a website to store data on their computer - especially if these data are collected and used by third parties without their knowledge - even if this does not normally have any further consequences. If this is the case, you have the option of blocking individual or all cookies or removing cookies that have already been stored. However, we would like to point out that this could impair your surfing experience on our websites.

4.3.1Blocking Third Party Cookies

Cookies from third parties are not normally required to use the internet offers you have requested. If you want to leave fewer traces on the internet, you should generally reject these cookies.

4.3.2Restriction of Tracking in Adobe Flash

It is recommended that you redefine the access protection settings using the online settings manager. However, Flash will no longer work 100% if you completely deny access.

LSO cookies (flash cookies) cannot be removed using the browser settings in the conventional way. To avoid having to manually remove them, you can download an extension for your browser that removes these cookies each time you close your browser.

4.3.3Delete unwanted cookies

If you do not want cookies, you can set your browser in such a way that you will be notified as soon as a cookie is to be set or that cookies are automatically rejected. However, if cookies are automatically rejected by the browser settings you have made, you will no longer be able to use certain functions of the website.
